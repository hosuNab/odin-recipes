Pasta Bolognese

Home

Pasta Bolognese is probably from Italy, but this is not. It's easy to make though and still delicious!

Ingredients

Bolognese sauce

  • 800 grams ground beef
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 2 onions
  • 3 carrots
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 800g tomato pasta sauce (2 cans)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • oregano

Served with pasta

  • 80 to 100g of pasta for each enjoyer

Steps

  1. Chop celery, onions and carrots into evenly sized small chunks
  2. Brown the ground beef in a large pan, season with salt and black pepper
  3. Move beef from the pan and set aside
  4. Add olive oil and soften the chopped vegetables in the pan
  5. When vegetables are softened, add garlic and the spices, cook 1 more minute
  6. Add tomato pasta sauce and groud beef back to pan
  7. Let simmer and reduce until you are too hungry
  8. Serve Bolognese sauce with a healthy amount of pasta
