Szechuan Beef

Home

Szechuan beef is a traditional Chinese recipe, but here we put our own twist to it by using ground beef, making it an ideal, cheap and easy week night dish.

Ingredients

For the stir fry

  • 4 eggs
  • 0,5 teaspoon salt
  • 0,25 teaspoon crushed white pepper
  • 0,5 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon Mirin
  • 2 tablespoons neutral oil
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 green/spring onion
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 0,25 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon sugar

Served with rice

  • 3 dl water
  • 1,5 dl jasmin rice

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the rice, it should cook while the sauce is made
  2. Cut tomato into chunks, chop onion and mince garlic
  3. Mix eggs, salt, sesame oilt and Mirin in a bowl
  4. Heat wok or stainless steel pan on medium heat
  5. When pan is hot add the 2 tbls oil and egg mixture right after, mix fast and don't let the eggs brown
  6. When eggs come together a bit, set aside from the pan
  7. Turn heat up to medium high and add 1 tbls oil, let heat up a bit
  8. Add tomato and vegetables to the pan and let soften
  9. When softened, add sugar and salt, and mix well
  10. Add eggs back into the pan and let heat under cover
  11. Check after minute or two, eggs should have fluffed up
  12. Mix up and let cook and reduce a bit, depending on how thick you want the sauce
  13. Serve with fresh cooked rice and enjoy!
